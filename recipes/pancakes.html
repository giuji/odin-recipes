Pancakes

Pancakes yay

stuff you'll need

  • 100g Plain flour
  • 2 Eggs
  • 300ml Milk
  • 1 Spoon of vegetable oil

steps:

  1. Put 100g plain flour, 2 large eggs, 300ml milk, 1 spoon vegetable oil and a pinch of salt into a bowl or large jug, then whisk to a smooth batter.
  2. Set aside for 30 mins to rest if you have time, or start cooking straight away.
  3. Set a crÃªpe pan over a medium heat and carefully wipe it with some oiled kitchen paper.
  4. When hot, cook your pancakes for 1 min on each side until golden, keeping them warm in a low oven as you go.
  5. Serve with your fav topping
