Popcorn

Popcorn yay

stuff you'll need

  • Popcorn kernels
  • Paper Bag
  • Microwave

steps:

  1. Add the popcorn kernels to a paper bag and fold over the the top twice.
  2. Microwave for about 2 to 4 minutes, stopping when you hear a few seconds between pops.
  3. Then, remove the bag from the oven.
